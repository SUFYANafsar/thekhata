<p>As the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) pushes forward with digital transformation, e-invoicing has become mandatory for many businesses across Pakistan. Under the latest regulations introduced through <strong>SRO 709(I)/2025</strong> and <strong>SRO 69(I)/2025</strong>, businesses are now required to generate electronic invoices and submit them directly to FBR’s <strong>IRIS system</strong> via <strong>PRAL (Pakistan Revenue Automation Pvt. Ltd.)</strong>.</p>



<p>If you&#8217;re unsure whether your business needs to comply, this article breaks it down clearly.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What Is the FBR E-Invoicing Mandate?</h2>



<p>FBR’s e-invoicing system is designed to ensure real-time tax documentation and eliminate fraudulent practices. Businesses must now generate invoices in a structured electronic format (JSON), embed a <strong>QR code</strong>, apply a <strong>digital signature</strong>, and <strong>submit the invoice through PRAL to IRIS</strong>.</p>



<p>This digital invoice acts as a legally recognized document for tax reporting, input claims, and audit purposes.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Which Businesses Are Required to Comply?</h2>



<p>If your business falls under any of the following categories, you are required to adopt <a href="/fbr-digital-invoicing-software/">FBR-compliant digital invoicing software</a>:</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. Manufacturers</h3>



<p>All registered manufacturers must issue digital invoices and post them in real-time to IRIS.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. Importers</h3>



<p>Import businesses are required to digitize their invoicing process for proper documentation and tax reporting.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. Distributors</h3>



<p>Distributors and wholesalers who supply products to retailers are covered under the e-invoicing rules.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. Wholesalers</h3>



<p>Bulk sellers and traders who distribute products in large quantities must comply with the mandate.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">5. Tier-1 Retailers</h3>



<p>Tier-1 retailers (as defined by FBR) are already required to implement e-invoicing systems for their daily transactions.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What You Need to Become Compliant</h2>



<p>To meet FBR’s requirements, your business must:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Use <strong><a href="/fbr-digital-invoicing-software/">FBR-approved e-invoicing software</a></strong></li>



<li>Ensure <strong>PRAL integration</strong> to post invoices to IRIS</li>



<li>Include a <strong>QR code</strong> and <strong>digital signature</strong> on each invoice</li>



<li>Maintain a secure record of all invoices for at least <strong>6 years</strong></li>
</ul>
