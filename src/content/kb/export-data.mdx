				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Overview</h2>
<ul>
<li>Export data list and reports in Microsoft excel from Splendid Accounts keep as a backup, or to access without logging in to Splendid Accounts.</li>
<li>Understand the items and file formats you can export and how.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>How it works</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>There isn’t a one-step feature to copy all of the company data. However, you can export data from individual areas of Splendid Accounts, and reports from our reports screen.</li>
<li>Our security means your company data is backed up and protected online so that it’s always available to access when you log in.</li>
<li>You’re responsible for keeping records of data entered in Splendid Accounts, so we recommend you regularly perform your backups in exported files.</li>
<li>Your user role determines the data you can export. The admin and owner user role lets you run and export all reports.</li>
</ul></div>
