				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Add other collection</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>In the Account menu, select other collection</li>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Other Collection”</strong></li>
<li>Enter the name of the other contact you have to collect money from. If you use multi-currency, you can enter your payment in the currency of the other contact.</li>
<li>If the other contact is not existing in the system you can directly add the contact right from the current screen by just writing the contact name and click enter, a form will be open for an additional contact you can save it and make a further transaction.</li>
<li><span style="color: var( --e-global-color-text ); font-family: var( --e-global-typography-text-font-family ); font-weight: var( --e-global-typography-text-font-weight ); font-size: 1em;">Select Account in which you want to record the collection of a a particular contact so that system will have a separate ledger for that contact against that contact.</span></li>
<li><span style="color: var( --e-global-color-text ); font-family: var( --e-global-typography-text-font-family ); font-weight: var( --e-global-typography-text-font-weight ); font-size: 1em;">Select Other Collection mode and appropriate fields depend on the payment mode. Enter the amount of the payment.</span></li>
<li>Click “<strong>Save and Continue Edit</strong>” to save the collection as a draft, or click “<strong>Save and Approve</strong>” to approve or click “<strong>Save and Pending</strong>” to mark the collection as pending and enter a new collection or click “<strong>Save and New</strong>” to record a new collection and save the previous one or click “<strong>Save and Close</strong>” to close the collection and save the collection in draft status.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Add-other-collection.jpg" alt="Add other collection" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Add-other-collection.jpg" alt="Add other collection" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<p><strong><u>Header Fields:</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Other Contact:</strong> Enter the name of the contact you would like to get money from. If you enter a name that&#8217;s not already a contact, Splendid Account adds it to your contact list.</li>
<li><b>Account:</b> Select Account in which you want to record the collection of a a particular contact.</li>
<li><strong>Date:</strong> This is set as default to the current date. You can change this also.</li>
<li><strong>Number:</strong> Use Splendid Accounts default numbering sequence or you can add your number sequence by clicking on number code and then a “+ “sign</li>
<li><strong>Reference:</strong> Choose a reference to suit your company. Later, you may want to search for your payment using your chosen reference.</li>
<li><strong>Master Group:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the payment in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Currency:</strong> If you have a Business Edition plan with multi-currency, you can select a foreign currency contact that you&#8217;ve already added, you can edit the exchange rate if required. If you entered a default currency contact , it automatically displays.</li>
<li><strong>Exchange Rate:</strong> This field will display if you select a contact of such currency that is different from your company’s currency so that you have to provide an exchange rate for the particular contact .</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><u>Line Item Fields:</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Other Collection mode:</strong> Select the payment mode by which you have to collect the amount from your contact . Different payment modes could be selected against a single contact .</li>
<li><strong>Accounts:</strong> Must be mandatory to select your accounts against payment mode in which account you would receive payment from your customer.</li>
<li><strong>Reference: </strong>Cheque number can be mentioned as a reference.</li>
<li><strong>Bank Name: </strong>You can enter the bank name of which cheque you are receiving.</li>
<li><strong>Instrument No. and Instrument date:</strong> Entered the instrument number, instrument date for record of your account. Must be mandatory to enter the instrument number, instrument date against <strong>Cheque</strong>, <strong>Draft,</strong> and <strong>Direct Deposit</strong></li>
<li><strong>Detail Group A:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the payments in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Detail Group B: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the payments in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Amount:</strong> Enter the amount you have to collect from your contact . He can either pay the full amount or partial amount accordingly.</li>
</ul></div>
