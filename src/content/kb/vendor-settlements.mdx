			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_1  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Overview:</h2>
<p>Record the Allocation of Payables against Paid payment of your supplier or vendors.</p>
<p><strong>Payable:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The vendor payments or debit notes you have to pay your vendor or supplier.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Paid</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The invoices, refunds, or credit note required to pay against your vendor payable.</li>
</ul></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_2  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Create Vendor settlement</h2>
<ol>
<li>In the left navigation menu select vendor settlement to settle the payable against paid payments.</li>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Vendor Settlement”</strong> to add settlements.</li>
<li>Complete the settlement form.</li>
<li>Click <strong>“Save and Continue Edit</strong>” to save the settle as a draft, or click <strong>“Save and Approve”</strong> to approve or click <strong>“Save and Pending”</strong> to mark the settlement as pending and enter a new settlement or click <strong>“Save and New”</strong> to record a new settlement and save the previous one or click “<strong>Save and Close”</strong> to close the settlement and save the settlement in draft status.</li>
</ol></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_3  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Vendor settlement field and their description</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vendor: </strong>Enter the name of the supplier you would like to get a refund. If you enter a name that&#8217;s not already a contact, Spartan Account adds it to your Vendor contacts.</li>
<li><strong>Date: </strong>This will default to today&#8217;s date. You can change this also.</li>
<li><strong>Vendor refund number: </strong>Use the Spartan Accounts default numbering sequence or you can add your customized number sequence.</li>
</ul></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_4  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Vendor settlement line fields</h2>
<ol>
<li>In Spartan Accounts default auto settlement has been made between payables and Paid.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Vendor-settlement-line-fields.jpg" alt="Vendor settlement line fields" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Vendor-settlement-line-fields.jpg" alt="Vendor settlement line fields" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<ol>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ol>
<li>Also, you can make manual settlement between your payables and Pay.</li>
<li>If you do manual settlement allocation between Payables and Paid should be equal, the leveraged difference between payable and paid allocation is not allowed.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Vendor-settlement-line-fields-2.jpg" alt="Vendor settlement line fields" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Vendor-settlement-line-fields-2.jpg" alt="Vendor settlement line fields" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
