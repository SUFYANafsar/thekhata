<h2>Quotes</h2>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Create a new quote or copy an existing one to outline the cost of work that a customer needs to be done. You can enter line by line details of the proposed quotation as well as attach any additional files, such as images or your businesses&#8217; terms and conditions. In the Sales menu, select Quotations.</p>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Sale Quotation</strong>”</li>
<li>Enter the name of the customer you&#8217;re sending the quote to. If you use multi-currency, you can enter your quote in the currency of the customer.</li>
<li>If the customer is not existing in the system you can directly add the customer right from the current screen by just writing the customer name and click enter, a form will be open for an additional customer you can save it and make a further transaction.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Type to search product </strong>and select the product, if it is not existing then you can enter the product directly the same as the customer name. Type the description of the product, then add the <b>Quantity and</b> <strong>Price</strong>.</li>
<li>You can add a line item discount and multiple taxes in a single product as per your needs.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit to</strong> save the quote as a draft, or click <strong>Save and Approve</strong> to approve or click <strong>Save and Pending</strong> to mark the quote as pending and enter a new quote or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new quote and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close the quote and save the quote in draft status.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Create-Quote.jpg" alt="Create Quote" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Create-Quote.jpg" alt="Create Quote" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<p><strong><u>Header Fields:</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ul>
<li><strong>Customer:</strong> Enter the name of the customer you would like to sell your products to. If you enter a name that&#8217;s not already a contact, Splendid Account adds it to your Customer list.</li>
<li><strong>Date:</strong> This is set as default to the current date. You can change this also.</li>
<li><strong>Expiry Date:</strong> Let your customer know the date you expect them to confirm their quote.</li>
<li><strong>Number:</strong> Use Splendid Accounts default numbering sequence or you can add your number sequence by clicking on number code and then a “+ “sign</li>
<li><strong>Reference:</strong> Choose a reference to suit your company. Later, you may want to search for your quote using your chosen reference.</li>
<li><strong>Master Group:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the quote in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Currency:</strong> If you have a Business Edition plan with multi-currency, you can select a foreign currency customer that you&#8217;ve already added, you can edit the exchange rate if required. If you entered a default currency customer, it automatically displays.</li>
<li><strong>Exchange Rate:</strong> This field will display if you select a customer of such currency that is different from your company’s currency so that you have to provide an exchange rate for the particular customer.</li>
<li><strong>Order Booker: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled. Let your customer know which order booker will go and collect the order. You can add multiple order bookers under the Sales Person menu.</li>
<li><strong>Sales Man: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled. This will display a list of all the salesmen you have entered into the system<strong>.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Delivery Person: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled. Select the delivery person of your choice for a particular customer, also you can choose based on areas.</li>
<li><strong>Add Quick/Scan Product:</strong> You can also Scan your multiple products directly from the Barcode scanner or enter a product name or Product number through the Add Quick product box.</li>
</ul>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/branches-header-feild.jpg" alt="Add Quick/Scan Product" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/branches-header-feild.jpg" alt="Add Quick/Scan Product" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<p><strong><u>Line Item Fields:</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Product: </strong>Select products if you have inventory products set up for your organization. Inventory products speed up data entry by automatically filling in the product field with information you&#8217;ve saved against your inventory products.<br />
If you enter a product that&#8217;s not already in inventory, Splendid Account adds it to your inventory.</li>
<li><strong>Product Description:</strong> Enter text as you&#8217;d like to describe the products, you&#8217;re sailing. You can include description lines in your product’s short description that will automatically display at line item.</li>
<li><strong>Quantity: </strong>Enter the quantity number of products you want to sell.</li>
<li><strong>Price:</strong> Splendid Accounts display the default product price that you have set in the product form or also you can enter your price.</li>
<li><strong>Last Sale Price:</strong> This display of you click in the price field, representing the last selling price of the product.</li>
<li><strong>Discount: </strong>Splendid Accounts has 3 different types of line item discount i.e.<br />
<strong><u>(1. Discount% 2. Discount in Amount 3. Discounted price per unit)</u></strong> you can enter your discount as per supplier agreement.</li>
<li><strong>Tax: </strong>Splendid Account has an option to add multiple Tax on products. If you have set up the product tax in product form default tax will be display. Or you can add additional tax into it.</li>
<li><strong>Detail Group A:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the quote in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Detail Group B: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the quote in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Amount: </strong>This is the calculated amount of customers have to pay against a product.</li>
</ul></div>

<p><strong>Discount in percentage:</strong> Gives % discount for an item</p>
<p><strong>Discounted price per unit: This</strong> Price will be considered as the main price for the line item rather than purchase price user set under the product page.</div>
