				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Create an order</h2>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Create a new order as per your customer needs. You can enter line by line details of the proposed order as well as attach any additional files, such as images or your businesses&#8217; terms and conditions.</p>
<ol>
<li>In the Sales menu, select the order</li>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Sale </strong><strong>Order</strong>”</li>
<li>Enter the name of the customer you&#8217;re creating the order If you use multi-currency, you can enter your order in the currency of the customer.</li>
<li>If the customer is not existing in the system you can directly add the customer right from the current screen by just writing the customer name and click enter, a form will be open for the additional customer you can save it and make a further transaction.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Type to search product </strong>and select the product, if it is not existing then you can enter the product directly the same as the customer name. Type the description of the product, then add the <b>Quantity and</b> <strong>Price</strong>.</li>
<li>You can add a line item <strong>discount</strong> and <strong>multiple taxes</strong> in a single product as per your needs.</li>
<li>Also last offered discount of invoice displays on sale order what discount you offered on sale invoice against the product and customer.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Sale-Order-Overview.jpg" alt="Sale Delivery Custom Template" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Sale-Order-Overview.jpg" alt="Sale Delivery Custom Template" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<ol>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit to</strong> save the order as a draft, or click <strong>Save and Approve</strong> to approve or click <strong>Save and Pending</strong> to mark the order as pending and enter a new order or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new order and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close the order and save the order in draft status.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Sale-Orders.jpg" alt="Sale Orders" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Sale-Orders.jpg" alt="Sale Orders" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<p><strong><u>Header Fields:</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Customer:</strong> Enter the name of the customer you would like to sell your products to. If you enter a name that&#8217;s not already a contact, Spartan Account adds it to your Customer list.</li>
<li><strong>Date:</strong> This is set as default to the current date. You can change this also.</li>
<li><strong>Delivery Date:</strong> Let your customer know the date the order will be delivered on.</li>
<li><strong>Number:</strong> Use Spartan Accounts default numbering sequence or you can add your number sequence by clicking on number code and then a “+ “sign</li>
<li><strong>Reference:</strong> Choose a reference to suit your company. Later, you may want to search for your quote using your chosen reference.</li>
<li><strong>Master Group:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the quote in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Currency:</strong> If you have a Business Edition plan with multi-currency, you can select a foreign currency customer that you&#8217;ve already added, you can edit the exchange rate if required. If you entered a default currency customer, it automatically displays.</li>
<li><strong>Exchange Rate:</strong> This field will display if you select a customer of such currency that is different from your company’s currency so that you have to provide an exchange rate for the particular customer.</li>
<li><strong>Order Booker: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled. Let your customer know which order booker will go and collect the order. You can add multiple order bookers under the Sales Person menu.</li>
<li><strong>Sales Man: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled. This will display a list of all the salesmen you have entered into the system<strong>.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Delivery Person: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled. Select the delivery person of your choice for a particular customer, also you can choose based on areas.</li>
<li><strong>Add Quick/Scan Product:</strong> You can also Scan your multiple products directly from the Barcode scanner or enter a product name or Product number through the Add Quick product box</li>
</ul>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/branches-header-feild.jpg" alt="Add Quick/Scan Product" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/branches-header-feild.jpg" alt="Add Quick/Scan Product" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<p><strong><u>Line Item Fields:</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Product: </strong>Select products if you have inventory products set up for your organization. Inventory products speed up data entry by automatically filling in the product field with information you&#8217;ve saved against your inventory products. If you enter a product that&#8217;s not already in inventory, Spartan Account adds it to your inventory.</li>
<li><strong>Product Description:</strong> Enter text as you&#8217;d like to describe the products, you&#8217;re sailing. You can include description lines in your product’s short description that will automatically display at line item.</li>
<li><strong>Quantity: </strong>Enter the quantity number of products you want to sell.</li>
<li><strong>Price:</strong> Spartan Accounts display the default product price that you have set in the product form or also you can enter your price.</li>
<li><strong>Last Sale Price:</strong> This display of you click in the price field, representing the last selling price of the product.</li>
<li><strong>Discount: </strong>Spartan Accounts has 3 different types of line item discount i.e.<br />
<strong>(1. Discount% 2. Discount in Amount 3. Discounted price per unit)</strong> you can enter your discount as per supplier agreement.</li>
<li><strong>Tax: </strong>Spartan Account has an option to add multiple Tax on products. If you have set up the product tax in product form default tax will be display. Or you can add additional tax into it.</li>
<li><strong>Detail Group A:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the order in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Detail Group B: </strong>This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the order in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Amount: </strong>This is the calculated amount of customers have to pay against a product.</li>
</ul></div>

<p>When a new product is created, there are two options available under the heading of “Do you sell or purchase in fractional units”.<br />
