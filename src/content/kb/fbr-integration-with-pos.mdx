				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><p>Spartan Accounts is now offering <span style="color: #333333;"><a href="/online-pos-fbr-integration/" style="color: #333333;">FBR Integration</a></span> with <span style="color: #333333;"><a href="/point-of-sale/" style="color: #333333;">POS system</a></span> with some pre-requisites steps need to be done in Spartan Accounts. You can now record your sales in a minute.</p>
<h2>Prerequisites:</h2>
<p>To enable FBR integration with Spartan Accounts, enable the checkbox available at company settings</p>
<ol>
<li>Enter the FBR POS Token that should be provided to you by FBR at POS settings under Company settings.</li>
<li>Enter the FBR POS Registration No at counter of POS on which you want to record the sales directly with FBR.</li>
<li>Enter the HS Codes of all products that you are selling.</li>
</ol>
<p>Once you updated all these things then you are ready to go to record your sales in minute and an invoice number will be generated from FBR as well.</p>
<h4>Warning:</h4>
<p>This is to inform you that if any product that has no HS Code saved at product detail and it is being sold, then no FBR Invoice No. will be generated as it is a requirement from FBR</p>
