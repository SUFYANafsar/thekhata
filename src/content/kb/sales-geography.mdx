				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><p style="text-align: left;">You can add Sales geography in Spartan Accounts categorized as regions, zones, areas, and subareas. You can assign the sub-area to customers and salesperson and depending on the selection of the areas you can easily filter the data at reports.</p>
<h2>Add Region, Zones, Territories, Areas, and Subareas</h2>
<h2>Regions</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Regions</strong>”.</li>
<li>Enter the name of the region.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit </strong>to save and continue to edit, or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new salesperson and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close it.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Zones</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Zones</strong>”</li>
<li>Select appropriate region</li>
<li>Enter the name of the Zone</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit to</strong> save and continue to edit, or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new salesperson and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close it.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Territories</h2>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add territories</strong>”</li>
<li>Select appropriate region and zone</li>
<li>Enter the name of the territories</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit </strong>to save it and continue to edit, or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new salesperson and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close it.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Areas</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add areas</strong>”</li>
<li>Select an appropriate region, zone, and territories</li>
<li>Enter the name of the areas</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit to</strong> save it and continue to edit, or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new salesperson and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close it.</li>
</ol>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>Subareas</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add subareas</strong>”</li>
<li>Select an appropriate region, zone, territories, and area</li>
<li>Enter the name of the sub-areas</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit to</strong> save it and continue to edit, or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new salesperson and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close it.</li>
</ol></div>
