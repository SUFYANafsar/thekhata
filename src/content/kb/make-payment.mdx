				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Overview:</h2>
<p>If you add or approve the bill, here’s some guidance on entering information into the fields.</p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_2  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Create Make Payment</h2>
<ol>
<li>In the left navigation menu select to make payment to pay vendor payments</li>
<li>Click <strong>“+Add Make payment”</strong> to add new payments.</li>
<li>Complete the make payment form.</li>
<li>Click <strong>“Save and Continue Edit”</strong> to save the payment as a draft, or click <strong>“Save and Approve”</strong> to approve or click <strong>“Save and Pending”</strong> to mark the payment as pending and enter a new payment or click <strong>“Save and New”</strong> to record a new payment and save the previous one or click <strong>“Save and Close”</strong> to close the payment and save the payment in draft status.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Create-Make-Payment-3.jpg" alt="Create Make Payment" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Create-Make-Payment-3.jpg" alt="Create Make Payment" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_3  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Make payments field and their description</h2>
<ul>
 	<li><strong>Vendor:</strong> Enter the name of the supplier you would like to pay. If you enter a name that’s not already a contact, Spartan Account adds it to your Vendor contacts.</li>
 	<li><strong>Date:</strong> This will default to today’s date. You can change this also.</li>
 	<li><strong>Make payment number:</strong> Use the Spartan Accounts default numbering sequence or you can add your customized number sequence.</li>
 	<li><strong>Reference:</strong> Choose a reference to suit your company or your supplier. Later, you may want to search for your purchase return using your chosen reference</li>
 	<li><strong>Group:</strong> This field appears only if you have set up Groups. Groups let you customize the differentiate of your vendor payment from different groups.</li>
 	<li><strong>Currency:</strong> If you have a Business Edition plan with multi-currency, you can select a foreign currency vendor that you’ve already added, you can edit the exchange rate if required.</li>
</ul>

If you entered a default currency vendor, it automatically displays.</div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_4  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Make payments line fields</h2>
<ol>
 	<li>Click on the payment mode searching box to add your payment mode to pay.</li>
 	<li>Click the Right icon to save the line item, or click the Cancel icon beside the line item to save or cancel the line item.</li>
 	<li>Use the Tab key on your keyboard to move between the lines.</li>
</ol></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_5  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Make payment line field and description</h2>
<ul>
<li>Payment mode: Select the payment by which mode you have to pay your vendor.</li>
</ul>
<p>Different payment modes could be selected against single payable.<br /><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Payment-mode.jpg" alt="Payment mode:" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Payment-mode.jpg" alt="Payment mode:" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts: Must be mandatory to select your accounts against payment mode from which account you would need to your vendor.</li>
<li>Document Number: Entered the document number for record of your account.<br />Must be mandatory to enter the draft number against Cheque, Draft, and Direct Deposit payments.</li>
</ul>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Document-Number.jpg" alt="Document Number" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Document-Number.jpg" alt="Document Number" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Detail Group A:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the differentiation of your vendor payment in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Detail Group B:</strong> This field appears only if you have enabled Groups. Groups let you customize the differentiation of your vendor payment in many ways.</li>
<li><strong>Amount:</strong>Enter the amount you have to pay against the payable invoices.</li>
</ul>
<p>Pay full amount or partial amount.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Account adjustment:</strong>Spartan Accounts has an account adjustment (optional) field to adjust your bills or enter the Tax rebate expenses in the Total bill.</li>
</ul>
<p>Or Add shipping charges of your goods received or services you take from your vendor or supplier.<br /><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Account-adjustment.jpg" alt="Account adjustment" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Account-adjustment.jpg" alt="Account adjustment" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_6  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Settlements</h2>
<ul>
<li>In Spartan Accounts, you have to option settle your payable bills against vendor payment.</li>
<li>Also, you can settle your supplier or vendor’s Debit note.</li>
<li>The default auto settlement of invoice and debit note has been settled against you returned amount entered.</li>
<li>Also, you can settle your payables manually you want to settle against payment entered.</li>
<li>Also, you have seen your adjusted amount and Balance amount in settlements.</li>
</ul>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Settlements-3.jpg" alt="Settlements" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Settlements-3.jpg" alt="Settlements" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_7  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
