				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Learn how to subscribe to your company license, Renew and Upgrade your users’ plan.</p>
<p><strong>Points to Remember:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>The subscription nearby to expire/end Spartan Accounts intimate you about your license expiry.</li>
<li>The payment date for your Spartan Accounts subscription depends on when you signed up for a subscription.</li>
<li>In Spartan Accounts, you pay your invoice using a bank deposit and a jazz cash merchant.</li>
<li>Pay your invoice using a debit or credit card. You can update your payment details at any time.</li>
<li>If you have subscribed Spartan Accounts outside Pakistan, then you are using the 2Checkout payment portal for license subscription.</li>
<li>If your subscription payment is overdue, your account might be suspended and you won’t be able to work on your registered company. To avoid this, you should make an immediate payment in Spartan Accounts.</li>
</ul></div>

<li>Subscription-based every month or Yearly basis over users’ plans.</li>
</ul></div>

<li><strong>Bank Deposit</strong></li>
<li><strong>Jazz Cash voucher</strong></li>
<li><strong>Jazz Cash Account</strong></li>
<li><strong>Credit /Debit card</strong></li>
