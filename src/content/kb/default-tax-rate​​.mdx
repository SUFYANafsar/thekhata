			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_1  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner">Splendid Accounts is facilitating a tax that is most commonly used in Pakistan i.e. General Sales Tax (GST). Although you can add more tax according to your needs.</p>
</div>
<div class="auto-format ui--animation">
<p><strong>Set up tax rates for your country</strong></p>
<p>To find tax rates, under <strong>Setup</strong> Menu select <strong>Tax</strong> in the <strong>General</strong> Column.</p>
<p><strong>The single tax rate for sales and purchases</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Identify the tax rate you need to pay and collect</li>
<li>You can update the default Tax on Sales and Tax on Purchases tax rates and enter your tax rate.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>The single tax rate for sales and purchases, with additional reporting requirements</strong></p>
<ol>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ol>
<li>Look at your last one or two sales tax returns to identify the reporting requirements you need.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<p>For example, your tax maybe 10% on all sales and purchases, but you’re required to report all sales to the government separately.</p>
<ol>
<li>Update the default <strong>Tax on Sales</strong> and <strong>Tax on Purchase</strong> tax rates and enter your tax rate of 10%.</li>
<li>You can add a new tax rate called <strong>Tax on Govt sales,</strong> with a single component sales tax at 10%.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Different tax rates based on the type of goods and services being bought and sold</strong></p>
<ol>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ol>
<li>Identify what taxes you need to collect or pay by looking at the sales and purchases you have made recently.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<p>For example, you may sell food and charge a 10% tax on cooked food, but 5% tax on uncooked food, while all of your purchases are at 5%.</p>
<ol>
<li>Add a new tax for <strong>Tax paid on Purchase</strong>s and enter your tax rate of 5%.</li>
<li>Add a new sales tax rate called <strong>Tax on cooked food</strong>, with a tax rate of 10%.</li>
<li>Add a new sales tax rate called <strong>Tax on uncooked food</strong>, with a single component of sales tax at 5%.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Different tax rates based on the location of your customers</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Identify what taxes you need to collect by looking at the tax you’re currently applying to your invoices.<br />
For example, you may sell to customers in your city (Lahore) at 9%, customers in your state (Punjab) at 8.25%, and customers out of your state at 7.25%, while all of your purchases are at either 9% or exempt.</li>
<li>Add tax for Tax paid on Purchases and enter your tax rate of 9%.</li>
<li>Add a new sales tax rate called Lahore sales tax with two components, City tax at 0.75% and State tax at 8.25%.</li>
<li>Add a new sales tax rate called Punjab sales tax with a single component of State tax at 8.25%.</li>
<li>Add a new sales tax rate called Out of state sales tax with a single component of Out of state tax at 7.25%.</li>
</ol></div>
