				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><p style="text-align: left;">You can add Sales Representatives in Spartan Accounts categorized as Delivery Person, Salesman, Order Booker. These representatives can be used in Sales transactions and will be helpful in sales reporting.</p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_2  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 style="text-align: left;">Add Delivery Person, Salesman, Order Booker</h2>
<p>The select Salesperson from the List column under the Setup menu.</p>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Add-Delivery-Person-Salesman-Order-Booker.jpg" alt="Add Delivery Person, Salesman, Order Booker" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Add-Delivery-Person-Salesman-Order-Booker.jpg" alt="Add Delivery Person, Salesman, Order Booker" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p>
<ol>
<li>Click “<strong>+Add Sale Person</strong>”</li>
<li>Enter the name of the Sale person</li>
<li>Choose the category for the sales representative. You can also select sub-area against a salesperson if sales geography is enabled for the company.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save and Continue Edit to</strong> save it and continue to edit, or click <strong>Save and New</strong> to record a new salesperson and save the previous one or click <strong>Save and Close</strong> to close it.</li>
<li>If the salesperson is not working with the company or temporary he is not available, you can also mark the salesperson as active or inactive.</li>
</ol>
<p><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Add-Delivery-Person-Salesman-Order-Booker-2.jpg" alt="Add Delivery Person, Salesman, Order Booker" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Add-Delivery-Person-Salesman-Order-Booker-2.jpg" alt="Add Delivery Person, Salesman, Order Booker" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_3  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 style="text-align: left;">Export file to excel</h2>
<p>You can export the list as excel or PDF format.<br /><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Export-file-to-excel-2.jpg" alt="Export file to excel" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Export-file-to-excel-2.jpg" alt="Export file to excel" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_4  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Assign sub-area to Sales Person</h2>
<p>You can select multiple sub-areas for your salesperson just by assigning the areas from the areas list.<br /><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Assign-sub-area-to-Sales-Person-2.jpg" alt="Assign sub-area to Sales Person" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Assign-sub-area-to-Sales-Person-2.jpg" alt="Assign sub-area to Sales Person" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_5  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2>Sales Person settings</h2>
<p>Spartan Accounts are letting you allow to set salesman, delivery person, or order booker on each page separately from company settings. You may also set these fields as required so that no transaction such as delivery, invoice, return, etc can not be approved until these selected.<br /><img data-lazyloaded="1" decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Sales-Person-settings.jpg" alt="Sales Person settings" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /><noscript><img decoding="async" class="center-kb-image" src="/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Sales-Person-settings.jpg" alt="Sales Person settings" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></noscript></p></div>
